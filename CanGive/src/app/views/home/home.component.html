About Us

CanGive is an Online charity focused on helping young families and those in need.

We provide free canned soup items to any one who uses our online services.

How to use ur services

There are three easy steps to using our services

  • Register - Register you email and password
  • Login - Log into your account - you will then be taken to a dashboard
  • Redeem token- in the dashboard you can redeem an item using your tokens. Simply select your desired token and click on the redeem button. You order will be requested
